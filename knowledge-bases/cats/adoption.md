# Cat Adoption Checklist

Before adopting a cat, prepare the home, choose a veterinarian, and plan a slow introduction period.

## Supplies

Useful supplies include food and water bowls, litter boxes, litter, a carrier, scratching posts, toys, a bed, nail clippers, and safe cleaning products.

## First Room

Start a new cat in one quiet room with food, water, litter, bedding, and hiding spaces. Let the cat explore the rest of the home gradually.

## Resident Pets

Introduce cats to resident pets slowly. Swap scents first, then use short supervised meetings. Do not force close contact.

## Safety

Remove toxic plants, secure loose cords, store medications safely, and make sure windows and balconies cannot be escaped through.
