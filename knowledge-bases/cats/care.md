# Cat Care Basics

Cats need consistent food, fresh water, a clean litter box, safe shelter, and daily attention from their humans.

## Feeding

Most adult cats do well with two measured meals per day. Kittens, seniors, and cats with medical needs may require a different schedule recommended by a veterinarian.

Wet food can help with hydration. Dry food can be convenient, but it should not replace access to fresh water.

## Water

Cats should always have clean water available. Some cats drink more from a fountain because moving water feels fresher to them.

## Litter

Provide one litter box per cat plus one extra box when possible. Scoop boxes daily and place them in quiet, accessible locations.

## Enrichment

Cats need scratching surfaces, hiding spots, window views, climbing space, and short play sessions. Wand toys and puzzle feeders are useful for indoor cats.
